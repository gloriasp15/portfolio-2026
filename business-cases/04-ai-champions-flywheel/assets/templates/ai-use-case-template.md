## Notes  

This template is designed to standardize how AI use cases are identified and evaluated across the organization.  

In a production environment, this template would typically be implemented as a **structured form (e.g. Power Apps)** integrated with **SharePoint repositories and automated workflows**, enabling centralized tracking, governance and scaling of AI initiatives.  

The level of detail required may vary depending on the complexity and risk level of the use case.

### 🧩 How it fits in the model

Within the AI Champions Flywheel, this template plays a key role in:

- **Capability Building** → structuring thinking and use case design  
- **Awareness** → making opportunities visible across the organization  
- **AI Discipline** → embedding governance and risk considerations early  

Use cases captured through this template feed into a **centralized repository**, enabling reuse, prioritization and scaling.

### ⚙️ Operational integration (Microsoft ecosystem)

While this repository provides a Markdown version of the template, in a real-world implementation it would typically be deployed as:

- A **Power Apps form** for structured input  
- Automated via **Power Automate workflows**  
- Stored in a **SharePoint repository** accessible to the AI community  
- Discussed and refined through **Microsoft Teams channels**  

This ensures seamless integration into the daily workflows of business teams.
