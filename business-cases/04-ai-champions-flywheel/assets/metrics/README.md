## AI Champions Flywheel - Effectiveness Metrics  

The effectiveness of the AI Champions Flywheel is measured through a focused set of KPIs capturing adoption, activation, capability and business impact.  

These metrics evaluate whether AI evolves from isolated initiatives into a **scalable, trusted and business-embedded capability**.

---

## KPI Design Principles  

The effectiveness metrics follow a set of guiding principles:

- **Adoption-centric, not tool-centric**  
  Metrics focus on behavioral change and business usage, not on tool deployment  

- **Outcome-driven over activity-driven**  
  The emphasis is on real impact (use cases, decisions, efficiency), not on number of initiatives  

- **Comparable over time**  
  Metrics enable before vs after tracking across adoption cycles  

- **Business-owned, AI-enabled**  
  Accountability sits with business teams; AI acts as an enabler  

- **Scalable and lightweight**  
  Metrics can be captured through existing workflows and community activity  

---

## A. Adoption & Community  

**A.1. AI Champions Coverage (%)**  
- **Definition:** % of business domains with at least one active AI Champion  
- **Formula:** (# Domains with ≥1 Champion / Total domains) × 100  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ High  
- **Value driver:** Distributed ownership  

---

**A.2. Active Champions Rate (%)**  
- **Definition:** % of Champions actively contributing  
- **Formula:** (# Active Champions / Total Champions) × 100  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ High  
- **Value driver:** Engagement & visibility  

---
**A.3. Community Engagement Level**  
- **Definition:** Level of interaction within AI community channels (Teams, Viva Engage)  
- **Formula:** # Posts + comments + reactions per month  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ Moderate  
- **Value driver:** Continuous visibility and peer-driven adoption  

---

## B. Use Case Activation & Scaling   

**B.1. Use Case Submission Rate**  
- **Definition:** Number of AI use cases identified by business teams  
- **Formula:** # Use cases / month  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ High  
- **Value driver:** Structured intake & awareness  

---

**B.2. Time-to-Activation**  
- **Definition:** Time from idea to first implementation  
- **Formula:** Avg. days (submission → deployment)  
- **Expected impact:** ↓ Significant  
- **Value driver:** Clear ownership & processes  

---

**B.3. Use Cases in Production (%)**  
- **Definition:** % of use cases successfully deployed  
- **Formula:** (# In production / Total submitted) × 100  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ Moderate  
- **Value driver:** Execution capability  

---

**B.4. Use Case Reuse Rate (%)**  
- **Definition:** Percentage of use cases replicated across multiple domains  
- **Formula:** (# Reused use cases / Total use cases) × 100  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ High (maturity indicator)  
- **Value driver:** Standardization and knowledge sharing  

---

## C. Capability  

**C.1. Training Completion Rate (%)**  
- **Definition:** % of employees completing AI training  
- **Formula:** (# Completed / Target population) × 100  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ High  
- **Value driver:** Structured upskilling  

---

**C.2. Self-Sufficiency Index**  
- **Definition:** % of use cases initiated by business teams  
- **Formula:** (# Business-led use cases / Total) × 100  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ High  
- **Value driver:** Decentralized capability

--- 

**C.3. AI Awareness Score**  
- **Definition:** Employee understanding of AI relevance and usage (survey-based)  
- **Formula:** Avg. survey score  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ Moderate  
- **Value driver:** Communication campaigns and storytelling  

---

## D. Governance & Trust  

**D.1. Use Cases Assessed (%)**  
- **Definition:** % of use cases undergoing AI risk assessment  
- **Formula:** (# Assessed / Total) × 100  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ High  
- **Value driver:** Embedded governance (AI Act alignment)  

---

**D.2. Model Traceability (%)**  
- **Definition:** Percentage of AI models registered and documented  
- **Formula:** (# Registered models / Total models) × 100  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ High  
- **Value driver:** Model registry and lifecycle management  

---

## E. Business Impact  

**E.1. Productivity Gains (%)**  
- **Definition:** Time saved through AI-enabled processes  
- **Formula:** (Time before – Time after) / Time before  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ Moderate to High  
- **Value driver:** Automation & decision support  

---

**E.2. Decision Cycle Time Reduction (%)**  
- **Definition:** Reduction in decision-making time  
- **Formula:** (Time before – Time after) / Time before  
- **Expected impact:** ↓ Significant  
- **Value driver:** Faster access to insights

---

**E.3. Executive Confidence Index**  
- **Definition:** Trust in AI-supported outputs for decision-making  
- **Formula:** Survey-based / qualitative scoring  
- **Expected impact:** ↑ Moderate  
- **Value driver:** Transparency, governance and consistent outputs
